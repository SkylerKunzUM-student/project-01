Shel Silverstein

Shel Silverstein is an author, poet, cartoonist, playwright, and songwriter. Silverstein is most widley know for his childrens book The giving Tree Shel was born on September 25th, 1930 in Chicago, Illinois. He claims his motivation to write started at a young age and continued the more he did it. His success on the book The Giving Tree opened up his career. He went on to write songs for johnney cash, Dr. Hook and Marianne Faithful. Silverstein died May 10, 1999 of a heart attack
